How It All Started

A Passion Turned into a Studio

Anika's Homemade Cake Studio was born from a simple love for baking and a desire to create something truly special for every celebration. What started as baking for family and friends in the heart of Ravet, Pune, quickly became a studio loved by the whole community.

Every cake is crafted from scratch with premium ingredients, designed with care, and delivered with love. We believe a cake isn't just a dessert — it's the centrepiece of your memory.

"Baked with Love, Purely Eggless." 🌸

Our Promise

The Eggless Pledge

🥚

Every single cake we make is 100% eggless. We understand that many families and individuals prefer egg-free options due to dietary choices, religious beliefs, or personal preference. You never have to ask or double-check — we simply don't use eggs. Period.
